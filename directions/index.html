Venue

The Workshop takes place at the NaWi at University Salzburg. You find the venue at Hellbrunnerstr.34 in Salzburg. Here is the local map folks. We love Salzburg!

How to reach the venue?

From the Trainstation:

If you travel to Salzburg by train you will arrive at Salzburg central station (Südtiroler Platz 1, 5020 Salzburg). From the central station you can take different busses to the venue. Here are some possibilities:

Take the Regionalbus 175 (direction: Rif Ortszentrum) until Salzburg Akademiestraße and walk in 10 min (750 m) to the venue.

Alternatively you can take the Stadtbus 22 (direction: Josefiau) until Salzburg Michael-Packer-Straße and walk just 150 m to reacht the venue.

From the Airport:

Travelling to Salzburg by plane will lead you to Salzburg Airport (SZG) located at Innsbrucker Bundesstraße 95, 5020 Salzburg. There are different possibilites to take the local transport from the airport to the venue starting from the bus station Salzburg Airport Terminal:

You can take the Obus 10 (direction: Zentrum Sam) until Salzburg Quartier Riedenburg (Neutorstraße), changing there to Salzburg Quartier Riedenburg (Moosstraße) in to the Stadtbus 22 (direction: Josefiau). Go out at Salzburg Michael-Pacher-Straße and walk 150 m to the venue.

Alternatively you can take the Obus 2 (direction: Hauptbahnhof-Obergnigl) to Salzburg Esshaverstraße and change there in the Stadtbus 11 (direction: Josefiau). Leave the bus at Salzburg Freisaal and walk to the venue in about 5 min (500 m)

Generell information for local transports:

You can find schedules and further information for local transports, as well as prices and tickets on the following link: https://salzburg-verkehr.at/
